OUR STORY

Bridging Knowledge & Practice

JASSAN Technologies and Electrical Services (JTES) is an innovation-driven technology company dedicated to advancing electrical, electronic, and digital solutions through research, education, and real-world application.

We specialize in STEM and STEAM education, robotics, AI, IoT, advanced electrical systems and critical thinking skills development — serving schools, universities, companies, industries, government institutions, NGOs and individual innovators across Ghana.
